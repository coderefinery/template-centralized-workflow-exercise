Bernaise sauce

Reduction:
1 tbsp white balsamico
1 tbsp water
5 crushed white peppercorns
1/2 shallot
-Mix in a pot, reduce to half volume, keep the liquid.

Sauce:
1 reduction (instruction above)
4 egg yolks
200 g butter

Spice sauce:
2 tbsp lemon juice
4 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
tarragon
parsley
